Meet Anita

Anita is the lovechild of three powerful projects - combining Amiya's simplicity, Aina's AI website generation, and Asta's markdown superpowers into one delightful CMS. No bloated frameworks, no Node.js nonsense - just pure vanilla JS magic with a FastAPI backend and SQLite database.

This isn't some corporate product - it's a passion project built by developers who just wanted something simple, cute, and not-WordPress.

What Makes Anita Special

Hybrid Content System

Switch between markdown and HTML modes depending on your needs. Write in beautiful markdown or craft pixel-perfect layouts - all in one editor.

AI That Actually Helps

Not just another "AI-powered" gimmick. Real assistance for writing, layout suggestions, and content generation that doesn't get in your way.

Lightweight AF

The entire codebase is smaller than your average node_modules folder. No bloat, no nonsense - just what you need to get stuff done.

Asta Markdown Editor

AI-assisted writing with full markdown support and real-time preview.

Aina Website Generator

Visual HTML builder with AI layout suggestions and live editing.
