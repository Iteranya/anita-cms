Anita CMS

Anita CMS is the perfect blend of simplicity and power, combining the best of Amiya's admin interface, Aina's AI website generation, and Asta's markdown editing into one lightweight package.

No bloated frameworks, no Node.js nonsense - just pure vanilla JS magic with a FastAPI backend.

Why Choose Anita?

  • Lightweight architecture (smaller than a React component's dependency tree!)
  • Hybrid content editing (switch between Markdown and HTML)
  • Built-in AI assistance for content creation

Aina Generator

Aina is your AI-powered website generator that helps you create beautiful, responsive websites without touching a single line of code (unless you want to!).

Get layout suggestions, color schemes, and even complete site structures with AI assistance.

Better Than WordPress

  • No security nightmares from outdated plugins
  • No bloated database slowing down your site
  • Real-time editing with instant previews

Asta Editor

Asta brings powerful markdown editing with AI assistance to your fingertips. Perfect for blogs, documentation, and any content-focused website.

Write in markdown, get beautiful formatted content with real-time preview and AI suggestions.

Blogging Made Simple

  • AI-powered writing assistance for better content
  • No more fighting with clunky WYSIWYG editors
  • Switch to HTML mode anytime for advanced customization
